## TFMs general overview


- Given a table with $n$ rows/instances/samples and $m$ columns/feature.
- All empty cells has to be further looked into.
- N/S means 'not specified' in that paper.
- \# means 'number of'
- SCM classes:
	- MLP-based: MLPs with varying dropout and non-linearities as computational maps.
	- Tree-based: either fitted or 'directly sampled'
